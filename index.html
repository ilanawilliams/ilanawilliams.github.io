Ilana Williams is a journalism and environmental science and policy major with photojournalism experience. She is familiar with reporting on local news and public interest stories. Her goal is work with National Geographic on covering the Climate Migration around the world.

Before starting college at the Univeristy of Maryland, Ilana took a gap year to Israel. She spent one semester studying at Hebrew University and the next semester volunteering in a youth villiage. During her time, she created a blog to highlight her experince. It is no longer running, but it sparked her interest in HTML/CSS. She wants to take future classes in HTML/CSS to learn how to apply it in her writing.

Ilana's interest in envrionmental science started during her freshman year of college. She did not know what field she wanted to write about like the students around her, but after her conversation with Rona Kobell, a well-known environmental justice reporter, she realized her options in environmental science.

When Ilana is not reporting or taking photos, she likes to paint with watercolors, swim and hang out with friends.
